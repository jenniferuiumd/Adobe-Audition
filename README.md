# **Adobe Audition: Professional Audio Production**
*The industry-standard audio editing and mixing software for podcasts, films, and music production.*



**Overview**

Adobe Audition is the professional choice for audio editing, mixing, and restoration. Used by podcasters, filmmakers, musicians, and audio engineers worldwide, Audition enables you to create everything from crystal-clear podcast episodes to sophisticated film soundtracks and music productions with precision and creative control.

---

**Why Choose Adobe Audition?**

- **Multitrack Editing**: Work with unlimited tracks for complex audio projects and seamless mixing
- **Advanced Audio Restoration**: Remove noise, clicks, hum, and other imperfections with AI-powered tools
- **Professional Sound Design**: Access comprehensive effects, filters, and processing tools
- **Spectrum Analysis**: Visualize and edit audio using advanced frequency displays
- **Multichannel Support**: Work with surround sound and immersive audio formats

---

**Key Features**

- **Essential Sound Panel**: One-click professional presets for dialogue, music, and sound effects
- **Auto-Ducking**: Automatically adjust background music levels during voiceovers
- **Batch Processing**: Apply effects and conversions to multiple files simultaneously
- **Sound Remover**: Eliminate unwanted sounds using machine learning
- **Parametric Equalizer**: Precise frequency control with visual feedback
- **Video Integration**: Edit audio while watching synchronized video playback

---

**Workflow Integration**

Adobe Audition works seamlessly with other Adobe Creative Cloud apps:
- Send sequences directly from **Adobe Premiere Pro** with native integration
- Import music and sound effects from **Adobe Stock** directly within the application
- Work with **Adobe Podcast** for enhanced speech processing
- Exchange files with **Adobe Premiere Rush** for mobile workflows

---

**System Requirements**

- **Supported Operating Systems**:
  - Windows 10 (64-bit) version 22H2 or later
  - Windows 11
